## Understanding IPC Compliance

IPC compliance represents adherence to a globally recognized framework of standards that govern the design, production, and inspection of electronic components. These standards define acceptable practices for soldering, assembly, handling, and environmental control.

### Key IPC Standards Integrated by Masline
- **IPC-A-610:** Acceptability of Electronic Assemblies  
- **IPC/JEDEC J-STD-033:** Handling and Moisture Control for SMD Components  
- **IPC/WHMA-A-620:** Requirements for Cable and Wire Harness Assemblies  
- **IPC-7711/7721:** Rework, Modification, and Repair of Electronic Assemblies  
- **IPC-A-600:** Acceptability of Printed Boards  

Each standard forms part of Masline’s quality foundation, ensuring components are safe, compliant, and suitable for high-performance applications.
